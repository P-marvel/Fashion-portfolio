The Designer

About Mr. Hammed Shogbamu Segun

With a strong foundation in fine arts and textile engineering, Mr. Hammed Shogbamu Segun brings a distinctive voice to contemporary fashion. At Olaks Fashion House, his work bridges the elegance of traditional craftsmanship with the boldness of futuristic design-creating garments that are both expressive and refined.

A graduate of Tobkas Fashion House, Mr. Segun has developed a deep expertise in fabric innovation, draping, and sartorial construction. Over the years, he has collaborated with prominent designers and contributed to collections that reflect both cultural heritage and modern aesthetics.

As Creative Director at Olaks, Mr. Segun continues to shape a new era of luxury fashion, where heritage meets imagination, and every piece carries a narrative.

Creative Process

01

Concept Development

Each Olaks collection begins with a deep dive into the worlds of art, architecture, and cultural evolution. This research forms the backbone of our creative process, inspiring unique narratives that are brought to life through carefully curated mood boards and original sketches. From concept to creation, every design reflects a thoughtful fusion of heritage and innovation, transforming inspiration into timeless fashion statements.

02

Material Exploration

At Olaks, material selection is both an art and a responsibility. We source sustainable, cutting-edge fabrics and partner with textile mills around the world to develop exclusive materials tailored to each collection. Every fabric is chosen not only for its visual and tactile qualities but also for its environmental integrity, ensuring our designs are as conscientious as they are captivating.

03

Pattern & Construction

Precision pattern-making meets experimental draping techniques. Our atelier combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology like 3D modeling.
