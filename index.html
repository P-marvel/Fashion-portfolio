The Designer

About Elara

With a background in fine arts and textile engineering, Elara brings a unique perspective to contemporary fashion design. Her work explores the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and futuristic aesthetics.

Graduating with honors from Central Saint Martins, Elara has since worked with leading fashion houses before launching her eponymous label in 2018. Her designs have been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and at Paris Fashion Week.

"Fashion should challenge perceptions while remaining wearable art. I strive to create pieces that tell stories through texture, form, and movement."

Creative Process

01

Concept Development

Each collection begins with extensive research into art, architecture, and cultural movements. Mood boards and sketches translate these inspirations into fashion narratives.

02

Material Exploration

We source sustainable, innovative fabrics and develop custom textiles through collaborations with mills worldwide. Every material is chosen for its aesthetic and environmental impact.

03

Pattern & Construction

Precision pattern-making meets experimental draping techniques. Our atelier combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology like 3D modeling.
